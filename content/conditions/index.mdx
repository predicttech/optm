# Conditions

Pain, stiffness, weakness, and reduced mobility can affect people of all ages. While these symptoms may appear in different parts of the body, they are often linked to underlying issues involving muscles, joints, tendons, ligaments, cartilage, or nerves.

At OPTM, we focus on understanding the root cause of these conditions instead of simply managing symptoms. Whether your discomfort is caused by injury, overuse, aging, inflammation, or muscle degeneration, identifying the condition early can help prevent long-term complications and improve recovery.
